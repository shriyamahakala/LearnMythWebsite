Resources

Here is a collection of various online resources that are helpful for learning more about Greek Mythology


Classical Mythology

This website is a supplement to the renowned and best-selling book, Classical Mythology. This website has many different resources for students and teachers.

Encylopedia Mythica

This award winning encyclopedia covers terms from mythology, folklore and even religion.



Encylopedia Greek Mythology

This is another really helpful encyclopedia that focuses specifically on Greek Mythology.






Greek Mythology

This website provides detailed descriptions about various Greek Mythology topics. There is also a complementary app available on the Apple App Store and Google Play Store.

Greek Mythology Link

This website, maintained by the Hellenic Studies Association at Brown University, provides access to the many myths that were retold by Carlos Parada.

Perseus Project

This website contains a very large collection of all sorts of resources. It contains material for everyone, from beginners to professionals.





Internet Sacred Test Archive

This website has the largest free archive of tbooks on folklore, mythology, religion and more.



Theoi Greek Mythology

This website aims to provide detailed descriptions of Greek gods, sprits, creatures and heroes.



World Myths and Legends in Art

This online resource, provided by Minneapolis Institute of the Arts, mainly provides pieces of work inspired by mythology to teachers.
